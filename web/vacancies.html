CONTRACT THROUGH SORBET

Picking the right agency for you

Dedicated Account Manager

Every contractor has a dedicated account manager, they will have experience of your role and industry. Your account manager knows how the site works and exactly what your role requires. Your account manager works to keep you working and will be your first point of contact on the phone.

CIS & UTR Payment options

We process payments for both LTD CIS registered contractors and UTR Sole traders. We are proud to have the lowest payment processing fee in the industry and no hidden fees or charges.

Rates

We value all the members of our team and pay competitive rates based on the role. We only employ quality contractors and we ensure that you’re on sites at the best possible rate. Sorbet are nothing without our trusted contractors; you will always be spoken to with respect from someone that has worked the role we’re offering.

No BS Policy

Sorbet Recruitment has a no BS policy that is at the core of our business and influences everything we do. We created Sorbet to fix a lot of the problems that have arisen with most agencies. Your account manager will always be straight and honest with you and we expect the same in return.

Sorbet Approved Status (SAS)

All contractors that maintain positive feedback over a 6 month period are then assessed by a Sorbet account manager on site and if successful are given SAS status across our network. SAS status means you can always be trusted to deliver and you’ll be given early access to new roles that are becoming available.

Working for you

We are here to work for you, we make sure you are paid correctly and on time and we always give you the best possible rates. Once you have received positive feedback your account manager will plan ahead to the end of the contract to try to make sure you are never out of work.
